Detailed Description

Defines the lifecycle status for services in the Service Locator.

Author
Synapse Framework Team
Version
2.0.0
Date
2025-09-12

This header provides a centralized, typed definition for all possible states a service can be in throughout its lifecycle. This is a key component of the state-aware Service Locator.

enum service_status_t

Enumeration of possible service lifecycle statuses.

Enumerator
SERVICE_STATUS_UNKNOWN 

The status is unknown or cannot be determined.

SERVICE_STATUS_REGISTERED 

The service has been created and registered, but not yet initialized.

SERVICE_STATUS_INITIALIZING 

The service is currently executing its init() and start() functions.

SERVICE_STATUS_ACTIVE 

The service is fully operational and ready for use.

SERVICE_STATUS_STOPPING 

The service is in its deinit() phase during a graceful shutdown.

SERVICE_STATUS_ERROR 

The service encountered a critical error and is not operational.
